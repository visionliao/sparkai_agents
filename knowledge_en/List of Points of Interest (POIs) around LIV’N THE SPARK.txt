Shopping

Distance: Approximately 1.3 km

Distance: Approximately 1.3 km
Supermarkets:
Lianhua Supermarket: Several stores of various sizes are located in the surrounding area.

Convenience Stores:
FamilyMart, Lawson, 7-Eleven: Multiple 24-hour stores are located downstairs and within a 500-meter radius.
Dining

Cafes:
Starbucks: Several locations in Jing'an Joy City and nearby office buildings.
M Stand, Manner Coffee, Peet's Coffee: All within walking distance.
Independent Specialty Coffee Shops: Various unique cafes are scattered along the nearby smaller streets.
Restaurants:
Fast Food: Chains like KFC, McDonald's, etc.
Chinese Cuisine: A wide selection from local Shanghainese dishes to Sichuan, Cantonese, and Xinjiang cuisine is available nearby.
International Cuisine: Japanese, Korean, Western, and Southeast Asian food can be found in Jing'an Joy City and the surrounding streets.
Local Delicacies: Many local noodle houses, dessert shops, and other time-honored or popular eateries are located around the apartment.
