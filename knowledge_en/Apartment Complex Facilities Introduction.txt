Building A, First Floor Common Area

The first-floor common area of Building A includes a variety of elevator facilities, functional rooms, and public spaces. Elevator facilities consist of three passenger elevators in the central part of the building, two observation elevators in Elevator Hall 2 on the north side of Building A, and one fire elevator in the fire elevator vestibule, primarily for emergency use.

Functional rooms include men's and women's restrooms in the central core, and a counter in the lobby area serves as an office for property management or service personnel. A mail/delivery room for packages and food deliveries is located near the entrance hall. A leasing office and a meeting room are situated in the central common area near the elevator hall; the meeting room is a "soft" space for relaxation, socializing, and waiting.

Public spaces feature a residential lobby as the main entrance and reception area, a lounge area with a sofa and coffee table to the right of the entrance hall, and a coffee bar to the right of the lobby offering self-service beverages. Additionally, there are two elevator halls on the north side and in the central part of the building. The fire stairs include Staircase 1, next to Elevator Hall 1, and Staircase 2, opposite Elevator Hall 2, both used for fire escape and daily access.

Building B, First Floor Common Area

Similarly, the first-floor common area of Building B offers elevator facilities, functional rooms, and public spaces. The elevator facilities include three passenger elevators in the lobby area for daily residential use and one fire/service elevator in the central fire elevator vestibule for emergencies and cargo transport.

Functional rooms comprise a counter in the lobby area for property management or service personnel and a mail/delivery room near the entrance hall for packages and food deliveries. Public spaces consist of the residential lobby, which serves as the main entrance and reception area, a lounge area with a sofa and coffee table in the entrance hall, and one elevator hall next to the building's main lobby. The fire stairs include Staircase 1, located next to the fire elevator room (primarily for fire escape), and Staircase 2, next to the elevator hall (for both fire escape and daily access).

7th Floor Common Area: Building A

The multi-function banquet area in Building A covers 50 square meters, with its core being a private banquet area featuring a long dining table for over 10 people, seamlessly connected to an open kitchen and an outdoor lounge. The open kitchen is equipped with a full range of cooking appliances and a large island bar with seating, serving both culinary and social functions. Additionally, there is a multi-purpose private room—a dining/card room—with an enclosed sofa group and a double-sided 60-inch TV wall, suitable for leisure, audio-visual entertainment, and card games. Adjacent to the private banquet area is an outdoor lounge, an open-air leisure space furnished with outdoor furniture.

7th Floor Common Area: Building B

The bar in Building B totals 150 square meters and features a bar area on the northeast side of the common area for drinks and informal socializing, which includes a bar counter and circular booth seating. A dining area is located on the south side, offering both long bar-style seating and large formal dining tables. On the northwest side, there is a KTV room, a private space for karaoke and audio-visual entertainment, furnished with a wrap-around sofa. The central service core includes a dry kitchen for preparing light meals and beverages, as well as two restrooms located near the bar.

7th Floor Common Area: Building A (Additional Facilities)

Building A also features a shared 350-square-meter fitness and yoga studio. The main training area is a comprehensive fitness core—the gym—which is divided into a cardio training area with treadmills and elliptical machines, a strength machine area with various fixed-track strength equipment, a free weight area with dumbbells and barbells, an open functional training area for comprehensive body training, and a core area for abdominal and core muscle workouts. Furthermore, there is a separate, quiet room—the yoga room—for yoga, Pilates, and meditation classes. Supporting facilities include fully-equipped men's and women's changing rooms (both with toilets, sinks, and lockers) and a drinking water fountain in the main gym area.

Additionally, Building A has a 300-square-meter shared activity and social space, divided into a work and focus zone and a leisure and creation zone. The work and focus zone includes a co-working space with individual workstations, shared long tables, and casual discussion seating; a quiet reading area with comfortable sofas for reading or informal conversations; a large conference room for formal meetings or client negotiations; and eight individual focus pods for deep concentration or private calls. The leisure and creation zone offers a game area with a pool table and lounge seating, a video game corner with a Switch and TV, a bar-style social area with a long activity table and bar stools for casual dining or conversation, a music corner with an electronic keyboard and seating, and two professional, soundproof VLOG live-streaming rooms suitable for video recording or high-quality meetings. The common facilities for this entire area include the main entrance hall and three restrooms.

7th Floor Rooftop Common Area (Connecting Buildings A and B)

The centerpiece of the open-air common area connecting the 7th floors of Buildings A and B is the shared rooftop garden pool. The main water facilities include a main pool with a total area of 130.2 square meters, measuring 15.5 meters long by 8.4 meters wide. It is subdivided into a shallow water area of 40.59 square meters with a depth of 1.2 meters, and a deep water area of 79.05 square meters with a depth of 1.3 meters. Additionally, there is a massage pool (jacuzzi) with a total area of 10.56 square meters, measuring 3.2 meters long by 3.3 meters wide, with a water depth of 0.8 meters. For safety, the pool area is enclosed by a 1.2-meter-high safety glass railing and is equipped with a comprehensive drainage system.
