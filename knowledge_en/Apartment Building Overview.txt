Project Profile: Xingya Plaza Phase II Rental Housing Project

The Xingya Plaza Phase II Rental Housing Project is located at Block 180, Yongxing Road, Baoshan Road Subdistrict, Jing'an District, Shanghai, and is being developed by the Jing'an District of Shanghai. In terms of design specifications, the project is classified as a Class 1 high-rise rental residential building with a Class 1 fire-resistance rating. The underground garage has a fire protection classification of Category I. The seismic fortification intensity is set at 7 degrees, and the structural system is a concrete frame-shear wall (with a seismic grade of 2). The designed service life is Category 3, 50 years. For waterproofing, both the basement and the roof are rated at Level I. The building consists of 2 underground levels, a South Tower with 21 floors above ground (building height of 78.00 meters), and a North Tower with 16 floors above ground (building height of 61.35 meters). The civil air defense project is a Class A civil defense facility, with a protection level of Nuclear 6 and Conventional 6. It serves as a garage during peacetime and as a second-class personnel shelter during wartime. The technical and economic indicators show a total land area of 8,292 square meters and a total construction area of 51,593.86 square meters, with 38,997.66 square meters of above-ground construction and 12,596.2 square meters of underground construction. The total floor area ratio-calculated construction area is 38,000 square meters, with a building footprint of 3,031.36 square meters. The project has a plot ratio of 4.58 and a building density of 36.56%. It is planned to have 293 motor vehicle parking spaces and 688 non-motor vehicle parking spaces. (Note: The number of floors mentioned here are the actual construction floors. As floor numbers such as 4 or 14 will be omitted in final operation, the actual floor count will differ from the operational floor numbering.)
