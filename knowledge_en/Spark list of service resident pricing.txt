V. Usage Regulations and Details

Cleaning Fee: An additional cleaning fee is required for private parties held in public areas. The standard fee is 200 RMB per event or 100 RMB per square meter in case of severe mess.
Catering Regulations: Food and beverages are prohibited in designated areas. A fine of 1000 RMB will be imposed for violations.
Damage Compensation: Any damaged items must be compensated for at their original price.
Overtime Billing: Overtime usage is charged by the hour. Any period less than one hour will be counted as a full hour.
Minors: Minors must be accompanied by an adult.
Check-out Time: The check-out time for all areas is 12:00 PM (noon).
Quiet Hours: The apartment's quiet hours are from 22:00 to 7:00 the next day.
Final Interpretation: The apartment management reserves the right of final interpretation of these regulations.
