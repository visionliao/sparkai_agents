1. Room Type Overview and Distribution

This project has a total of 579 apartment units. Among them, the Studio Deluxe, with a gross floor area of 42-48 square meters, totals 22 units, all located on floors B2 to 16 of Building B. The Studio Executive, with a gross floor area of 50-69 square meters, has a total of 360 units, distributed between Building A (344 units, floors A2-25) and Building B (16 units, floors B8-18). The Studio Premier, with a gross floor area of 67 square meters, totals 12 units, all located on floors A8-21 of Building A. The One Bedroom Deluxe, with a gross floor area of 58-88 square meters, has 150 units, distributed between Building A (46 units, floors A8-25) and Building B (104 units, floors B2-19). The One Bedroom Premier, with a gross floor area of 77-99 square meters, has 19 units, with 1 unit on floor A2 of Building A and the remaining 18 units on floors B3-19 of Building B. The Two Bedrooms Executive, with a gross floor area of 108 square meters, totals 15 units, all located on floors A8-25 of Building A. The Three-bedroom apartment, with a gross floor area of 134 square meters, is a single unit located on floor B19 of Building B.

3. In-Room Facilities and Item Checklist

Studio Apartments (Studio Deluxe, Studio Executive, Studio Premier): The standard configuration in the kitchen and dining area includes: 1 Whirlpool refrigerator, 1 Whirlpool range hood, 1 Whirlpool microwave, 1 Whirlpool washer-dryer combo, 1 Whirlpool induction cooktop (in some units), 1 Panasonic electric kettle, 2 sets of tableware/glassware/cutlery, 1 dining table, 2 dining chairs, 1 pot, 1 spatula, 1 set of Zhang Xiao Quan knives, 1 shoe cabinet, and 1 trash bin. The living area has: 1 projector with remote and 1 projection screen (in some units), 1 storage cabinet, 1 sofa, 1 coffee table, 1 router, and 1 set-top box with remote. The bedroom is furnished with: 1 1.5-meter or 1.6-meter bed frame, 1 mattress, 1-2 bedside tables, 1 bedside lamp, 1 set of curtains, 1 safe, 6 clothes hangers, and 1 set of bedding. The bathroom is equipped with 2 bath towels, 2 face towels, 1 trash bin, 1 hair dryer, and a washlet (in some units). Other configurations include 1 full-length mirror, a Trane air conditioning system, and Legrand switches and sockets.

One-Bedroom Deluxe Apartments: The configuration is largely similar to the studio types but with differences in space and some furniture. The living room adds 1 Sharp TV with remote, 1 TV cabinet, 1 two-seater sofa, 1 ottoman, 1 round coffee table, and 1 set of curtains; the router and set-top box with remote are the same. The master bedroom bed frame is upgraded to 1.8 meters, with 2 bedside tables, 1 bedside lamp, and 1 chest of drawers; other furnishings are the same. The kitchen, dining, and bathroom configurations are similar to the studio types.

Two/Three-Bedroom Apartments: These build upon the one-bedroom layout with additional bedroom and bathroom configurations. The second bedroom is equipped with 1 1.5-meter bed frame, 1 mattress, 1 bedside table, 6 clothes hangers, 1 set of bedding, and 1 chest of drawers. The second bathroom is equipped with 2 bath towels, 2 face towels, 1 trash bin, and 1 washlet. The number of dining chairs is increased to 4. The three-bedroom apartment adds another bedroom configuration on top of the two-bedroom layout.

4. Public Utilities and Services

Utilities: Cold water is priced at 6 RMB per cubic meter, subject to fluctuations based on the monthly standard of the water supply bureau. Hot water is 45 RMB per cubic meter. Residential electricity is 1.2 RMB per kilowatt-hour, subject to fluctuations based on the monthly standard of the power supply bureau. Air conditioning electricity is 0.7 RMB per kilowatt-hour.

Services: The project provides a once-a-week housekeeping service for all apartments.

5. Other Public Facilities and Equipment

Elevators: Both Building A and Building B are equipped with 3 passenger elevators and 1 service/fire elevator each. Additionally, Building A has 2 scenic elevators.

6. Other Information

Swimming Pool: The tentative opening hours for the swimming pool are from July 1st to August 31st, daily from 8:00 to 18:00. Available time slots for booking are 8:00-10:00, 10:00-12:00, 12:00-14:00, 14:00-16:00, and 16:00-18:00.

Parking: An underground garage is located on the B1 and B2 levels. The parking fee is 10 RMB per hour, with a daily maximum of 80 RMB. A monthly parking pass is 1000 RMB, for a non-fixed parking space. The total number of parking spaces is 293, with 158 on the B1 level and 135 on the B2 level. For prospective clients visiting the property, parking is free for the first hour via an online voucher; any time exceeding one hour will be charged.
