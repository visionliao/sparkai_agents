使用规定与细则 / Terms and Conditions of use
I.公共区域如未复原可能会产生额外清洁费用（单个区域清洁费200元；单个严重污染家电清洁费100元）
If public areas are not restored, extra cleaning fees will apply (200 yuan per area; 100 yuan per severely polluted appliance).
II. 可以提前联系前台确认和预约场地，每个单位同时有限于一个预订
You may contact the front desk in advance to confirm and reserve the venue, Only one reservation is allowed per unit at the same time.
III. 需要收取押金3000元，如有物品损坏需要照价赔偿
A deposit of 3,000 yuan is required, Compensation is required for any damaged items at the actual price.
IV. 预定的使用时间外的超时费不满一小时按一小时计算
Overtime charges incurred beyond the reserved usage period will be billed in hourly increments, with any partial hour being rounded up to the nearest full hour.
V. 6人以上的活动自动被视为私人派对
Any activity with more than 6 people is automatically considered a private party.
VI. 公寓宁静时间为晚上10点至白天7点
The quiet hours in the apartment are from 10 p.m. to 7 a.m.
V. 公寓拥有最终解释权
The apartment has the final interpretation rights.
